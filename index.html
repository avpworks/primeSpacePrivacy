Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2025

Welcome to Prime Space, an immersive rendering application for exploring 360-degree architectural visualizations, including on Apple Vision Pro.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Credentials: Users log in using credentials provided via Firebase Authentication.
  • Project Data: Each account is linked to personal architectural renderings hosted securely in Firebase.
  • Device Info: Anonymous device and performance data may be collected to improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate users and display their assigned project renders.
  • To enable a smooth and personalized immersive experience.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is securely stored in Google Firebase. Communications are encrypted via HTTPS, and access is controlled through Firebase Authentication.

4. Project Privacy

Your architectural renders and project data are private. Other users cannot view or access your content unless explicitly authorized. Each account is securely isolated using Firebase Authentication.

5. Demo Access

Users can explore a sample version of the app without submitting personal information. No user data is collected during demo use.

6. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Delete your account and associated data

To submit a request, please contact us at team@avpworks.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material updates will be announced in-app or via email.
